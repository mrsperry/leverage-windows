About Us

Established in 2019, Leverage Windows was borne out of necessity for a client-driven, consultative approach to window and door solutions. Leverage Windows is proud to be made up of professionals that are passionate about the glazing industry, and that are providing a jolt of energy and enthusiasm to an ever-aging industry. Proudly serving all of South Florida, from Palm Beach through Monroe counties, Leverage Windows brings professional knowledge to all your window needs.

Our services include free detailed estimates, shop drawings, permitting, and turnkey installation services. From high-end luxury homes to commercial buildings, no project is outside of our area of expertise. Contact us today for a free consultation on your project.

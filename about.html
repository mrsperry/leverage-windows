About Us

Established in 2019, Leverage Windows was borne out of necessity for a client-driven, consultative approach to window and door solutions. Leverage Windows is proud to be made up of professionals that are passionate about the glazing and construction industry.

We offer a variety of services, such as the following: windows/doors, glass and cable railings, glass wine enclosures, skylights, structural steel stairs, and more. Proudly serving all of South Florida, from Palm Beach through Monroe counties, Leverage Windows brings professional knowledge to all your window, aluminum, and steel fabrication needs. No project is too large, nor too small.

We provide free detailed estimates, shop drawings, permitting, and turnkey installation services. From high-end luxury homes to commercial buildings, no project is outside of our area of expertise. Contact us today for a free consultation on your project.
